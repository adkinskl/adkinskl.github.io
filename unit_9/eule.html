EULE BOUTIQUE

Logo & Identity

For this project I developed an identity system for a company I made up, based on the word ‘Eule,’ meaning owl in German. After the initial research phase I decided to create Eule Boutique, a vintage thrift shop. I established a target audience and began building the branding and identity for my company. My final designs include a logo, stationary, clothing tags and tote bags for the boutique.
